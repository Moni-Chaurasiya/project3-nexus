    About Us

    CampusExtraVaganza Events started with a purpose to provide ‘ONE STOP SOLUTIONS’ for all your Marketing- related worries. customer or staff, Craftworld Events assures to handle every part of it with finesse. We step in right at the stage of Idea Generation to its flawless execution..

    Every project is approached with an artistic passion because we love what we do. We develop ever-lasting relationships with our clients to help build a strong foundation on which creative thinking and heart felt design can add value to their businesses and invoke their vision.

    We pride ourselves on providing the best service and crafting communication materials that bring together rich ideas and intelligent design. Our passion for design and art is what strives us to make every clients design needs stand out among the rest with our added unique and personal touches.

    WHAT MAKES CAMPUSEXTRAVAGANZA EVENTS DIFFERENT

    We are not an “event designer” or a “bridal consultant” – we will never tell you what your event should look like/be like/cost, unless of course you ask. We work with clients to help them create the event that they want, with as little stress as possible. This means that we will probably encourage you to simplify things so on the day of the event you can put your stress aside and enjoy your event, be it a gaming events, conferences, festivals, organization party or trade shows event.
